# Bug 003 — Sitemap: What's new - the link on the event "Sundaes on the Pier" doesn't active


---

## Bug Summary
**Category:** Malfunction  
**Severity:** Low  
**Occurrence:** Always (can be reproduced at all times)  
**Environment:** Windows 11, Chrome  
**Created on:** Tuesday, April 15, 2025 at 12:07 PM

---

## Steps to Reproduce
1. the user goes to the **Sitemap** (the bottom of the [Ben & Jerry’s homepage](https://www.benjerry.co.uk/) );
2. the user clicks on **What's new** title;
3. the user scrolls down until 2022 year, April;
4. pay attention to the line **"Sundaes on the Pier"**.

---

## Expected Result
The line **"Sundaes on the Pier"** should contain the clickable hyperlink, according to the structure of the **"What's new"** section.

---

## Actual Result
The line **"Sundaes on the Pier"** doesn't clickable.
